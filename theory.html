Music Theory

An important part of learning music is learning to read music.

The pictured note is the fourth C on the piano, also known as middle C. It’s easily distinguishable by the single line that goes through it. On the treble clef, it is located on the line under the bottom line. On the bass clef, however, middle C is located on the line above the top line.
